Privacy Policy

Website

We collect the following personal information:

  • IP address, browser information, and other technical details about the means of connection with our website
  • If you register an account, a username and password, along with an optional email address, are also stored

This information is used to authenticate users in our control panel, send notification emails relating to your services, and maintain the security of our platform. We will only disclose your personal information to the extent that we are required to do so by law, or in connection with ongoing or prospective legal proceedings. If information is provided to third parties within the terms of this privacy policy, we will notify you unless legally obligated not to. We do not share information with any advertisers or affiliates.

Virtual machine data

We will never access your virtual machine data without your permission unless required to do so by law. If you explicitly grant us permission to access your virtual machine, we will only use such access to assist you with open support tickets.
